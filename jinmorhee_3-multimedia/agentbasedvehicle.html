The visitors will be simulated using vehicles. It shows two types of vehicles: the connoisseur and the kid. The connoisseur is an art expert and wants to make the best of her time at the museum, exploring and analyzing the artwork. She has the artwork exhibited as a list of attractors. When she gets too close to a specific piece, she will eliminate that piece from her list of interests. The kid is not particularly interested in art, but in playing. He will try to move fast everywhere, avoiding other vehicles and all the artwork. The kid will keep playing for a certain time and after getting tired, he will exit the museum.
